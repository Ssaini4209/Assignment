Gummies!

Candy is symbol for happiness.You are happy when you get some candy.But so much happier when you can give it to others

For decades, people of all ages have valued candies as a treat that they can indulge in. Early versions of these delicious confections can be found in the remains of ancient civilizations where honey and fruits were blended. These days, sweets come in a beguiling variety of forms, hues, and flavours, each of which offers a distinct sensory experience. There is a candy for every taste, from traditional hard sweets that break down gradually on the tongue to chewy gummies that deliver a delicious sweetness explosion and melt-in-your-mouth chocolates that conjure complete happiness.
